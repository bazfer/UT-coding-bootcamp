About Me

To create a sense of wonder and joy, that's why I became a video game developer.

In my six years of experience I have had the opportunity to wear many hats, from Artist and Designer to Community Manager. With a diverse toolkit, I have found a niche in the role of Producer, serving my teammates by clearing and paving the path ahead, allowing them to careate their magic.

I have a special affinity and a good share of experience for the free-to-play, game-as-a-service business model. The community involvement in the game development that comes with it opens the gates to new, never-done-before reciprocal interactions between devs and players.
